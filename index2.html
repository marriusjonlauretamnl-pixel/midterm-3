Introduction

Resident Evil is a popular survival horror franchise created by Capcom. It is known for its intense gameplay, dark atmosphere, and thrilling storylines that involve deadly viruses, dangerous creatures, and secret organizations.

The series follows different characters as they try to survive outbreaks caused by the Umbrella Corporation, a powerful company responsible for creating bio-weapons. These outbreaks turn people into zombies and other terrifying monsters.

Some of the most well-known characters in Resident Evil include Leon S. Kennedy, a skilled government agent, Claire Redfield, a brave survivor, and Ada Wong, a mysterious spy with unclear motives.

Over the years, Resident Evil has expanded beyond games into movies and other media, making it one of the most iconic horror franchises in the world.

Trivia

  • Leon S. Kennedy: First appeared in Resident Evil 2 (1998) as a rookie police officer.
  • Ada Wong: Mysterious spy whose true motives are often unclear.
  • Claire Redfield: Chris Redfield’s younger sister and a skilled survivor.
  • Chris Redfield: Veteran BSAA agent, strong and determined fighter against bio-weapons.
